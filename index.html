Frequently Asked Questions

Can you place a porta potty on a city sidewalk?

This often requires a permit from the city. We can advise you on the process, but the client is typically responsible for securing any necessary permits for public placement.

What is the best restroom solution for a downtown pop-up shop?

To match a professional retail environment, a small, elegant restroom rental trailer is the ideal choice. It signals quality and provides a superior customer experience.

Do you partner with municipalities or non-profits?

Yes. We view ourselves as civic partners and are proud to work with public and non-profit organizations to provide essential sanitation infrastructure for our community.

How do you service units on a busy highway construction site?

Our drivers are trained in work zone safety and coordinate with site managers to schedule service during times of lower traffic, ensuring the safety of our team and your crew.
